Interactions

Use the W key to interact with objects:

  • Pick up an object: Face the object and press W
  • Drop an object: Press W while holding an item
  • Place onions in the pot: Face the pot and press W
  • Deliver a dish: Drop the dish into the delivery area using W

Cooking Process

  1. Collect three onions from the onion pile, one by one
  2. Place the onions in the pot
  3. Wait for the timer to count down
  4. Retrieve a plate
  5. Collect the cooked soup from the pot
  6. Deliver the dish to the delivery area

Each delivered dish gives you and your partner one point. Coordinate your actions to maximize points!
