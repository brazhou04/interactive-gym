You will be playing a game called Slime Volleyball against an opponent. The goal of the game is to score against your opponent and prevent them from scoring against you.

Use the arrow keys to control your character. To score a point, the ball must hit the ground on your opponent's side of the field. It is separated by a small black field post which you cannot cross, shown below.

You will play 30 rounds of the game with the same partner. You will receive a bonus payment for your score in the game of $0.05 per point that you score, up to $1.50. To earn any bonus, you must remain active in the game.

After a few moments on this page, the start button will turn green. When you are ready to begin, press it. If there is no player already waiting, you will join a waiting room. If there is a player already in a waiting room, the game will begin.

Do not navigate away from the page! Switching to another tab or opening other windows may degrade performance and impact your experience as well as that of other players.
